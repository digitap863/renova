About

Renova Maria

Renova Maria is a distinguished full-service interior design and production company nestled in the heart of Dubai

Understanding the essence of functional and aesthetically pleasing commercial spaces is our forte. We collaborate closely with businesses to create environments that inspire productivity and leave lasting impressions.

From cozy apartments to luxurious villas, we cater to diverse residential needs. Our team crafts personalized design solutions that reflect the unique tastes and lifestyles of our clients. Whether it's a subtle refresh or a complete overhaul, our expertise in renovations ensures a seamless transformation. We manage the entire process, from conceptualization to execution, with precision.

At Renova Maria, excellence is at the core of everything we do. Our team is dedicated to delivering unparalleled craftsmanship, meticulous attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. We prioritize our clients' visions and preferences, working collaboratively to bring their ideas to life. Clear communication, transparency, and a deep understanding of individual needs define our approach.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering inspired designs that fulfill client expectations through collabration and exceptional service. Our goal is always to create designs tailored to our clients’ unique personal styles and practical needs. Much of our business comes from repeat clients and direct referrals. Once people hire us, they seem to want to do it again!.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating interiors that reflect our client's personality and lifestyle, by capturing their design dreams and making them a reality. We are dedicated to listening to our clients in a professional manner and carrying out the design process until the desired product is achieved.

Our Vision

Our Vision aims to shape spaces with “timeless designs” and want to be a globally recognized professional interior design company, providing solutions in the field of high-end corporate, commercial & residential interiors.
